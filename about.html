Name origin

My real name is hard to pronounce for many people so I decided to use rhebok instead. It's the name of an animal that many people don't know about. This was an attempt at spreading awareness of its existence. However, it backfired and I am now often jokingly associated with the shoe brand that is also named after the animal.

Here's a short description of the animal from its Wikipedia page :

The grey rhebok or grey rhebuck, locally known as the reebok in Afrikaans, is a species of antelope native to South Africa, Lesotho, and Eswatini. The specific name capreolus is Latin for 'little goat'.

Computer science

I have always been very interested in programming : I remember reading an Introduction to Java e-book on my first Android smartphone on the subway while commuting to my middle school. Ever since then I've been going in that direction for many different reasons, but mainly because I love it.

Today I know several programming languages, however I don't think that I've mastered any of them, the same as I don't think that I've mastered any of the natural languages that I can speak.

I know how to create websites, from scratch or by using frameworks. I am also acquainted with Node.js. I have had my fair share of database from PostgreSQL to MySQL. I have unfortunately had to use PHP before. I have ventured into the depths where I learned Racket and Objective-C. I spent countless hours trying to figure out why I was getting seg faults but then I discovered C++. And of course, last but not least, I know Python.
