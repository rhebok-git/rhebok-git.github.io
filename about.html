Name origin

My real name is hard to pronounce for many people so I decided to use rhebok instead. It's the name of an animal that many people don't know about. The reason for this choice was an attempt at spreading awareness of its existence. However, this backfired and I am now often jokingly associated with the shoe brand that is also named after the animal.

Here's a short description of the animal from its Wikipedia page :

The grey rhebok or grey rhebuck, locally known as the reebok in Afrikaans, is a species of antelope native to South Africa, Lesotho, and Eswatini. The specific name capreolus is Latin for 'little goat'.
